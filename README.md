## Brand Identity

- **Name:** DY Dimsum (Dimsum Yanti Lubis)
- **Tagline:** Handmade Premium
- **Colors:** Red (#C41E3A), Gold (#D4A017), Dark (#1A1A2E)
- **Location:** Medan, North Sumatra, Indonesia
- **Target:** Middle to upper-middle class, ages 18-45

## Business Highlights

- **Initial Investment:** IDR 85,000,000 (~$5,300 USD)
- **Break Even Point:** 8-10 months
- **Revenue Target (Month 6):** IDR 45-60M/month
- **Strategy:** Online-first via GoFood, GrabFood, ShopeeFood
- **Unique Selling Point:** Medan-Oriental fusion (Andaliman Dimsum, Arsik Siumai)
