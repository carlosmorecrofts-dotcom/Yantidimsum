About DY Dimsum

Premium handmade dim sum, crafted with love from Yanti Lubis's kitchen in Medan

Our Story

DY Dimsum was born from Yanti Lubis's passion for Oriental cuisine. Starting from a home kitchen in Medan, we now serve premium handmade dim sum that combines traditional Cantonese recipes with unique North Sumatran flavors.

Every piece is handcrafted using fresh ingredients selected daily from local markets. No preservatives, no excess MSG — just pure, natural deliciousness.

With innovations like Andaliman Dimsum and Arsik Siumai, we proudly offer flavors you won't find anywhere else.
